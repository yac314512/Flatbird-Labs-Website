Capabilities

Four layers. One operating system for AI-search visibility.

i.

Priority Roadmap.

Every gap converts into a ranked action — quick wins (Reddit seeding, FAQ rewrites) versus major plays (creator series, earned media placement) — scored by impact-to-effort and weighted by source-tier authority. Other tools surface the data. Opteros delivers a ranked, executable roadmap and tracks what each move does to the visibility score week over week.

A 0–100 measurement of how often, how positively, and how authoritatively a brand is cited across the four LLMs, benchmarked against named competitors in the category.

Corroboration Audit

Maps tier-one editorial, tier-two community, and tier-three creator content to surface the third-party gaps that make a brand uncitable inside any of the four LLMs.

iv.

Query Sandbox

Live testing of any natural-language query against all four LLMs side-by-side, citations exposed. The CMO sees exactly what the customer sees in real time.
